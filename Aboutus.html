About Us

COC GradeHub is a student–teacher consultation system designed to streamline grade management and communication. It provides students and teachers with a centralized platform to view, request, and manage academic grades efficiently.

Our Mission

To enhance the academic experience by providing a simple, intuitive platform that fosters transparency, communication, and timely grade consultations between students and teachers.

Key Features

  • View and request grades
  • Submit and clear grade requests
  • Teacher feedback and comments
  • Centralized dashboard for easy access
